Rules

Discord Rules

Text-Based Chat Rules
These rules will apply in all text-based discord channels, a violation will result in a warning and temp-mute in case of repeated offense.
  1. PG13: The server is PG13 and all chat must be. Profane or mature language is not allowed in chat.
  2. Chat Abuse: All abusive chat usage is not allowed. This includes spamming, begging, encourage others to spam and self-advertising.
  3. Common Sense: Use channels for what they are meant for, e.g. talking about marriage invites in #general is not allowed.
  4. Equality: All players/users are equal, you are expected to treat all players with equality and respect. Hence all kind of racism, hatred, cyber bully, toxic behaviors, etc is not allowed.
  5. Honesty: Players are expected to provide accurate information to staff members. e.g. “I maybe xraying” is not allowed.
Voice-Based Chat Rules
These rules will apply in all voice-based discord channels, a violation will result in a warning and temp-mute/temp-ban in case of repeated offense.
Applicable text-based rules also apply.
  1. ChatBot Abuse: Abusing any bot/text-to-speech to play inappropriate sounds is strictly not allowed. e.g. Using Rythm Bot to play "Somebody Once told me" memes.
  2. Voice Chat Abuse: Speak super loudly and using voice changers etc are not allowed.

Minecraft Rules

Game Play Rules
These rules will apply on our Minecraft server, a violation will result in a warning and temp-ban in case of repeated offense.
  1. Cheats: All kind of hacked clients, plugins and mods that gives a player unfair advantage over others will not be tolerated. e.g. Fly Hack
  2. TP-Killing: We define tp-killing as requesting other players to teleport to you and kill them intentionally or unintentionally afterward.
    If both players are able to come to an agreement on the matter, the staff team will ignore the issue, if not, the killer will receive warn/temp-banned if he returns the items and longer ban/perm ban if otherwise. e.g. "Tpa for free diamonds!"
  3. Server Griefing: Usage of lag machines, exploits, and bugs, etc to harm the server in any way are not allowed, the player will be warned/temp-banned and the machine/mechanism used will be forcefully removed.
  4. Player: Any methods of trapping, controlling or exerting control over another player, even including hacking into their accounts, is not allowed. Simple threatening or anger-driven actions that are reasonable will result in a warning, while players on second-offense and higher will get temp-muted or temp-banned. e.g. Nether Portal Traps, Bed Killing.
Chat Rules
These rules will apply on our Minecraft server's chat, a violation will result in a warning and temp-mute in case of repeated offense.
  1. Chat: All applicable discord text-based rules apply.
  2. Begging: Asking for items, money or ranks, etc in public chat with/without a target person repeatedly (x10+) within a period of 5 minutes is regarded as begging and it's not allowed.
  3. Command Abuse: Abusing commands that you have or have bought is not allowed. e.g.Impersonating staff members using the /nick command.
